Evolutionary and conservation genomics

The Hohenlohe lab is part of the Department of Biological Sciences and the Institute for Bioinformatics and Evolutionary Studies (IBEST) at the University of Idaho. Our research focuses on the genomic architecture of evolving populations with applications to conservation.
